Advanced Web Developer

From £40,000 per year

We are seeking a talented and experienced Web Designer to join our team. As a Web Designer, you will be responsible for creating and implementing visually stunning, user-friendly, and responsive websites for our clients.

Responsibilities:
• Collaborate with clients and team members to develop website design concepts that align with clients' goals and brand identities.
• Create wireframes, mock-ups, and prototypes to communicate design ideas to clients and team members.
• Design and develop responsive and engaging user interfaces for websites, web applications, and mobile apps.
• Ensure designs are properly implemented by working closely with developers and quality assurance teams.
• Stay up to date with the latest design trends, tools, and technologies to continuously improve our design processes and outputs.

Requirements:
• Bachelor's degree in graphic design, web design, or a related field.
• Proven experience in designing and developing visually appealing and user-friendly websites.
• Strong skills in design software such as Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, Figma, or similar.
• Proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
• Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written.
• Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously while meeting tight deadlines.
• Strong attention to detail and a commitment to producing high-quality work.
• A portfolio of web design work showcasing your skills and design style.


We are an equal opportunity employer and welcome applicants from all backgrounds. We offer a supportive and inclusive work environment, ongoing training and development opportunities, and competitive compensation and benefits packages.
If you are a highly motivated and talented Web Designer with a passion for creating innovative and effective web designs, we would love to hear from you.

• Develop wireframes, mock-ups, and prototypes for web projects.
• Work with developers to ensure that designs are accurately implemented.

We are an equal opportunity employer and welcome applicants from all backgrounds.
